A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hey there! Welcome to my page.
I'm Joanna, a senior at Clarmeont Mckenna College studying psychology with a leadership sequence.
I was born and raised in Orange County, California. I don't plan on staying, but when I do leave,
the one thing I'll miss for sure is the food scene.

My hometown is full of great restaurants that serve a beautiful array of dishes.
You can find practically anything here, from durian to birria tacos to Korean BBQ. There's always
something new to try!

(Plus, my family is Korean, so you bet food is their love language!)
It goes without saying at this point that I love food.
I love consuming it, cooking with it, and watching others do the same.
I encourage you to rethink the way you see and enjoy food. Eating is more than an act of survival.

  • Kimchi Mac n Cheese

    • ~ I listed this one first for a reason. I made some for my boyfriend and his friends and they were all huge fans.
    I've always liked mac n cheese, but most of the time, it's too rich; the cheesiness gets boring after a while.
    This recipe is awesome because you're adding kimchi, Korean fermented cabbage.
    Kimchi complements the richness of the cheese nicely and gives the whole dish a nice spicy kick. Kimchi is also good for your gut,
    so you have one more reason to try it!

  • Golden Fried Rice

    • ~ Fried rice is already awesome on its own, but coating your rice in egg and tumeric elevates it to a whole new level.
    If you want to make your fried rice taste like the one you order at a restaurant, here's a secret: get your pan smoking hot!
    Once it's smoking, then add your rice. The grains will crisp instantly, giving it a smokier flavor.
